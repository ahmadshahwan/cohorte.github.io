Resilient Component-based Software Framework

COHORTE is a platform for developing and rolling out robust and reliable software. Software produced with COHORTE has a high level of robustness and reliability since its is made up of a range of components and services protected from one another by isolation containers. This modular architecture guarantees that software cannot be paralyzed in the event of failure of one of its components.
